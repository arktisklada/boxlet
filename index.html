Why?

I'm always running out of space on my phone.
I want to know that my photos are safe.

Boxlet comes from the idea that backing up a phone's photos should be easy, private, and free.

Massive online hacks? No thank you.
Being nagged to pay for more space? No please.

Features

 

Sync

Sync all photos at once or selectively sync chosen photos

 
 

Flashback

See your photos from yesteryears

 
 

View and save your backed-up images

 
 

Pair with your own server

Store your photos directly on your own server or on Amazon S3

 

Requirements

Cloud server with ssh and sudo access

  • Ruby 2.x
  • MongoDB 2.4+
  • ImageMagick
  • iOS 7.1+
